Are You...

Burlesque brought my passion for theatre and dancing together and with a touch of sexy, I was able to create unique worlds blending different eras and ideas. As a result I am also able to spread the message that our sexuality is not something that we should be embarrased about. It is what makes us human and denying such freedom of expression is denying a part of of who we are. For this reason I am also part of an LGBTQ Burlesque Group called QWN.

...Magic

Magic on the other hand, fascinates me in the way in which it bends reality and creates such moments of awe that touch people and bring about a catharsis. I sometimes do magic in conjunction with magic through I often perform them seperate as well. I love performing magic that tells a story as well as comedic ones because nothing heals us more than a good laugh. I incoporate many different characters in my magic from fairies to dark sexy witched but my favorite one is Urusla.

Ursula Delacroix

Ursula Delacroix is an 18th C. French vixen with a British accent. Though she is originally from Paris, she got married to a British Duke and moved to the English countryside. While residing there she killed off her obnoxious husband and inherited all his money. With her newfound freedom she got increasingly bored. As a result she took to magic and burlesque. She occasionally will secretly sneak out of her Estate so as to avoid her servants’ gossiping to perform at seedy establishments with questionable patrons and show of her impeccably wicked skills. She has built quite the reputation.
