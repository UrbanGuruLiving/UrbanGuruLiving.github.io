Curious...

Dancing is something that has always been a part of me. An expression of my existence. A free-spirited dancer, dancing to the sound of her soul. I've been fortunate enough to have the honor to perform at big festivals like EDC, as a back up dancer for an artist, for huge private parties, in music videos and in big thetrical shows. I also perform with props, such as veil fans and am a fire performer skilled in fire fans, which I love combining with belly dancing.
