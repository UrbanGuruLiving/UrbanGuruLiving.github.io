To Learn...

My background in acting ranges from local theatre to abstract narrative pieces. The idea of entering the psyche of another human or being excites me. I feel like Syd in Legion, living the life of another person like it was a dream. Most of my training was done in Los Angeles though I've bee performing in plays since I was five. I love playing the most insane character because of the freedom of expression it provides for me. Comedy is another thing I'm naturally good at and I think it's because of the weird face people tell me I make.

Characters

Thanks to my wild imagination and obssession with nerdy things like Lord or The Rings, Anime, vampires and all things fantasy, I often get hired to be a character in an immersive experience or event like E3. I've was a fairy at Jareth's Labyrinth, a demon at Universal studios and I've even sat inside a glass display in bodypaint pretending to read a book.

"All the world's a stage and we are its actors" as the famous Shakespeare wrote in his play "As You Like it." Acting for me is escaping this lucid dream and entering another one in which I am the creator. Forget about boundaries and rules. You are what you choose. A fairy, an alien or an explorer of truths.
