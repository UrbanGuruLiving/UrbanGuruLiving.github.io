CatHouse

15/06/2019 Poetry Sensual

In an indigo of lust,
Eyes wandering to dust,
Velvet curtains on the wall,
Spiral stairs forsaken halls.
As my red lips drip of blood,
They ignite the fire fought,
In a battle oh so cold,
To suppress the tender soul.
And as the music drowns the screams,
And the masks conceal the greed,
I am lost inside your eyes,
That seduce me 'till I burn.
And for that eternal second,
The music ceases to exist,
And the strawberries turn into,
An orgasmic color stream,
That explodes like thin confetti,
On my silken woven skin.
